REPORT ON INTER-SCHOOL COMPETITION "FELICIA" "21"
Won First Place in Interschool Competition

The students of Marigold International School, Kengeri, Bangalore participated in FELICIA 21, an inter-school competition organized by NPS, Banashankari, Bangalore, on 18th, 19th and 20th November 2021 on MS Teams virtual platform. Our school students participated enthusiastically in many art & cultural events like Rhythmania, Art from the Heart! Yogabhyuday and Cadence Crush. It was a wonderful opportunity to exhibit their skill and creativity. The efforts of our students reaped rich results in the form of prizes.
